Organize your pantry 



            A well-organized pantry starts with regularly updating your inventory to 
            keep track of items and their expiration dates. By uploading and 
            maintaining a digital record of your pantry, you can easily monitor what
            you have, avoid food waste, and plan meals efficiently. This system ensures
            that expired products are removed in time and helps you restock essential
            items before they run out.
